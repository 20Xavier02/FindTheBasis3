Interactive Transformation

Moves: 0

Timer: 2 seconds

How to Play

This game helps you understand linear transformations:

  1. The blue point represents your starting vector
  2. The red point is your target
  3. Drag the green basis vectors (i' and j') to create a transformation
  4. Click "Go" to see where your transformation sends the blue point
  5. Try to make the transformed point (light blue) land on the red point!
