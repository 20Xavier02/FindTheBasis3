How to Play

This game is about understanding linear transformations:

  1. The blue point represents a vector in 2D space
  2. The red point is your target
  3. Drag the green basis vectors (i' and j') to create a transformation
  4. Click "Go" to see where your transformation sends the blue point
  5. Try to make the blue point land on the red point!
